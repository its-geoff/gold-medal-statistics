WHAT IS GOLD MEDAL STATISTICS?

Gold Medal Statistics is an innovative way to measure athletic performance. We take data from the World Athletics Scoring Tables to compare your team's marks and track individual improvement.

WHY GOLD MEDAL STATISTICS?

This is a unique program that allows you to find athletes' strengths and areas of improvement. By scoring all of their marks using a standardized system, you will be able to determine which events they are best at, whether or not those events are of the same type.
